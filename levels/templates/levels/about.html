About this Game

This is a treasure hunt game based on the theme of the Seven Wonders of the World can be a fun and challenging activity that requires a range of soft skills to solve. The game can be designed to incorporate clues and challenges related to each wonder, requiring participants to use problem-solving, communication, and critical thinking skills to progress.

There are a total of 8 levels of which 7 levels are the questions related to the Seven wonders and to have some fun, one level is added extra in the middle
