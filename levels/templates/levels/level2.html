Petra - Jordan

Petra is an ancient city located in the southwestern region of Jordan, carved into sandstone cliffs that glow pink and red in the light of the sun. It is considered one of the most significant archaeological sites in the world, dating back to the 4th century BC. The city was the capital of the Nabataean Kingdom and was an important center for trade and commerce. Petra is a testament to the creativity and engineering prowess of ancient civilizations, and is a must-see destination for history buffs and adventurers alike.
